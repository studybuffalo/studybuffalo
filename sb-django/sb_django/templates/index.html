What are Study Buffalo?

We are two polyurethane foam rubber buffalo. While we are unusual representatives, we work hard to improve the delivery of health care services, by improving both the practioners and technology supporting them.

To help the next generation of pharmacists, and other health profesisonals, we provide a unique selection of study tools, guides, and quizzes. For the particulary motivated student, we also offer monetary bounties to those who wish to update older resources or develop new resources.

Tools

Every once in a while, a new technology, an old problem, and a big idea turn into an innovation. --Dean Kamen

While technology does not define a good practioner, we feel it can definitely make their job easier. We have developed tools to solve the real problems practioners face in their day-to-day to make the care they deliver that much better. All tools are designed to be easy-to-use, so take a look and see what you can use to improve your practice today.

All our tools would be nothing without the technology behind them. We are strong supporters of open source software development and are happy to share all our source code and software under a GNU GPLv3 License. All source code is available on our GitHub profile.
