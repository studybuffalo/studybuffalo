Data Extraction

Studybuffalo.com is a website designed to provide practice tools and information to pharmacists in Alberta (and anywhere else where they may be value). Some of our tools take available resources and redesign them into user-friendly interfaces that provide unique functionality and information. To power these tools we often will extract data from publically available databases, such as yours, to incorporate into our systems.

Web Crawlers

The aforementioned data extraction is powered by various web crawlers designed with Python running on an Ubuntu server. To ensure minimal impact on your own severs we try to ensure the following:

  • All web crawlers will identify themselves and link to this page
  • We will comply with your sites robots.txt file (looking for it in your root directory automatically)
  • We try to limit our downloads and data extraction to times and days where it is unlikely to be disruptive for your regular users. Additionally, we try to only run the web crawler no more than once a week where possible.
  • We only make one copy of the required data. We use this copy to parse and extract the required data and then host it on our own servers. Our users will not be using your bandwidth and other resources.

Contact Us

While we hope that our above precautions are sufficient, we understand if you have concerns with our web crawlers and data use. We will oblige any requests to cease operations, but do hope that you will contact us to discuss the issue prior. These tools are currently being used to help deliver patient care by numerous pharmacists and we hope that alternative arrangements can be agreed upon to meet the needs of all parties involved.

Please feel free to contact us at studybuffalo@studybuffalo.com with any questions, comments, or concerns.
