Equations & Calculations

Calculations used to generate above data. Due to rounding, some numbers may not add up exactly. The actual calculations are all rounded at the final step, where as these ones are rounded between each step to make them easier to follow.

Patient Weight Calculations

\begin{align} IBW\ (Male) & = 50\ kg + \left(\frac {0.9055\ kg}{cm} * height\ (cm)\ over\ 152.4\ cm\right) &\\[10pt] IBW\ (Female) & = 45.5\ kg + \left(\frac {0.9055\ kg}{cm} * height\ (cm)\ over\ 152.4\ cm\right) & \end{align}

Patient CrCl Calculations

\begin{align} CrCl\ (Male) & = \frac{\left(140 - age\right) * weight}{SCr} * 1.2 &\\[10pt] CrCl\ (Female) & = \frac{\left(140 - age\right) * weight}{SCr} & \end{align}

Empiric Dose Calculations

\begin{align} Loading\ Dose & = \frac{25\ mg}{kg} * actual\ body\ weight &\\ \end{align} \begin{align} Maintenance Dose & = \frac{15\ mg}{kg} * actual\ body\ weight &\\ \end{align}

Empiric Steady State Calculations

\begin{align} k_e & = \left(0.00083 * CrCl\right) + 0.0044 &\\ \end{align} \begin{align} t_{1/2} & = \frac{ln(2)}{k_e} &\\ \end{align} \begin{align} t_{SS} & = t_{1/2} * 4.32 &\\ \end{align}

Patient-Specific ke Calculation

\begin{align} \Delta t & = Trough\ Draw\ Time\ - Peak\ Draw\ Time\ &\\[10pt] \end{align} \begin{align} k_e & = \frac{log\left(Peak\ Level\ (mg/L) - Trough\ Level\ (mg/L)\right)}{\Delta t\ (h)} &\\[10pt] \end{align} \begin{align} t_{1/2} & = \frac{ln(2)}{k_e} &\\ \end{align}

Patient-Specific Cmax Calculation

\begin{align} \Delta t & = Peak\ Draw\ Time - End\ of\ Infusion &\\[5pt] & = \left(Peak\ Draw\ Time - Infusion\ Start\ Time\right) - Infusion\ Duration\ (h) &\\[10pt] \end{align} \begin{align} C_{max} & = \frac{Peak\ Level\ (mg/L)}{e^{-k_e * \Delta t}} &\\[10pt] \end{align}

Patient-Specific Cmin Calculation

\begin{align} \Delta t & = Next\ Infusion\ Start\ Time - Trough\ Draw\ Time &\\[5pt] & = \left(Infusion\ Start\ Time + Dosing\ Interval\ (h)\right) - Trough\ Draw\ Time &\\[10pt] \end{align} \begin{align} C_{min} & = Trough\ Level\ (mg/L) * e^{-k_e * \Delta t} &\\[10pt] \end{align}

Patient-Specific Volume of Distribution Calculation

\begin{align} V_d & = \frac{dose\ (mg)}{C_{max}\ (mg/L) - C_{min}}\ (mg/L) &\\[10pt] \end{align}

Patient-Specific ke Calculation

\begin{align} \Delta t & = C_2\ Draw\ Time - C_1\ Draw\ Time &\\[10pt] \end{align} \begin{align} k_e & = \frac{log\left(C_1\ Level\ (mg/L) - C_2\ Level\ (mg/L)\right)}{\Delta t} &\\[10pt] \end{align} \begin{align} t_{1/2} & = \frac{ln(2)}{k_e} &\\ \end{align}

Patient-Specific Cmax Calculation

\begin{align} \Delta t & = C_1\ Draw\ Time - End\ of\ Infusion &\\[5pt] & = C_1\ Draw\ Time - Infusion\ Start\ Time - Infusion\ Duration\ (h) &\\[10pt] \end{align} \begin{align} C_{max} & = \frac{C_1\ Level\ (mg/L)}{e^{-k_e * \Delta t}} &\\[10pt] \end{align}

Patient-Specific Cmin Calculation

\begin{align} \Delta t & = Dosing\ Interval\ (h) - Infusion\ Duration\ (h) &\\[10pt] \end{align} \begin{align} C_{min} & = C_{max} * e^{-k_e * \Delta t} &\\[10pt] \end{align}

Patient-Specific Volume of Distribution Calculation

\begin{align} V_d & = \frac{dose\ (mg)}{C_{max} - C_{min}} &\\[10pt] \end{align}

Empiric Volume of Distribution Calculation

\begin{align} V_d & = 0.7\ L/kg * weight\ (kg) &\\[10pt] \end{align}

Empiric Dose Calculation

\begin{align} dose & = V_d * (Desired\ Peak - Desired\ Trough) &\\[10pt] \end{align}

Empiric Pharmacokinetic Parameter Calculations

\begin{align} k_e & = \left(0.00083 * CrCl\right) + 0.0044 &\\[10pt] \end{align} \begin{align} t_{1/2} & = \frac{ln(2)}{k_e} &\\[10pt] \end{align}

Empiric Interval Calculation

\begin{align} \tau & = \frac{log(Desired\ Peak\ (mg/L)/ Desired\ Trough\ (mg/L))}{k_e\ h^{-1}} &\\[10pt] \end{align}

Patient-Specific New Dose Calculation

\begin{align} dose & = V_d * (Desired\ Peak - Desired\ Trough) &\\[10pt] \end{align}

Patient-Specific New Interval Calculation

\begin{align} \tau & = \frac{ln(Desired\ Peak\ Level\ (mg/L)) - ln(Desired\ Trough\ Level\ (mg/L))}{k_e} &\\[10pt] \end{align}

New Dose Cmax Estimate

\begin{align} C_{max} & = \frac{Dose\ (mg)}{V_d\ (L) * \left(1 - e^{-ke * \tau}\right)} &\\[10pt] \end{align}

New Dose Cmin Estimate

\begin{align} C_{min} & = C_{max} * e^{-ke * \tau} &\\[10pt] \end{align}
